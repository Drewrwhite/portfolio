About Me:

My name is Drew (he/him), and I currently live in Portland, Oregon. I am a full time student, studying Data Engineering at Epicodus, a tech bootcamp.

While I do have some experience working with tech in basic sense on my own time, I have never worked in the tech industry. I currently own a barber shop and have been barbering for close to 10 years. I have recently stepped back to be just an owner and manage things within my business. Having had a desire to work in the tech industry for years, I decided to use the opportunity of my status change within my barber business, to pursue those dreams!

In my free time, I enjoy making and listening to music, doing Danzan Ryu Ju-Jitsu, and spending time in the beautiful outdoors, exploring with my wife and two daughters.

Steps to Success:

  1. Be Passionate

    2. - Love what you do and take pride

  2. Be Persistent

    3. - Things are going to be difficult but don't give up

  3. Have a beginner mindset and be open

    4. - Always strive to learn and look at things like you can always learn more and be better

  4. Set reasonable goals

    5. - Don't set expectations that are out of reach

  5. Stay focused

    6. - Don't lose vision of what your goals are

  6. Nourish yourself

    7. - If you are not rested mentally and physically, you cannot properly stay focused

  7. Give yourself credit

    8. - Be humble but you deserve credit for your hard work
